Dan was looking out the window one morning when he saw a bright red dragon fly by! He was shocked, and ran to the window, shouting 'Look, a dragon!' But no one got up from their desk, as no one believed poor Dan. Defeated, Dan sat back down, tears beginning to form. But then, another dragon! This time bright blue! Instead of telling everyone what he saw, Dan ran to the roof with his camera, excited to catch the dragons in flight.
